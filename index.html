About Me:

Hello and thank you for visiting my page! My name is Sandra and I am brand new to the coding world. I am a female of color and a first generation Mexican-American. My parents are both immigrants that came to the US in the hope of one day providing their children with a life filled with the opportunities that they never had growing up. Thanks to their sacrifices I now have the opportunity to pursue intersts, such as art, the outdoors, education, and now coding! I now have hobbies that my parents never even knew existed, such as paddle boarding, camping, and painting.

My professional background is primarily in the field of Early Childhood Education. I started off as a Paraprofessional while I was in college, working for the Los Angeles Unified School District and the Moreno Valley Unified School District. From there I got my first job teaching right after graduation at Limerick Elementary School as an Early Childhood Special Education Teacher. Since then I have also worked as an ECEAP Early Childhood teacher in Vancouver, WA, a Head Start teacher in Portland, OR, and as an Education Site Manager. More recently I have embarked on a journey to face my fears and challenge myself by attending the Epicodus coding bootcamp. My skills include, but are not limited to:

  • Speaking both English and Spanish fluently.
  • Management of multiple teams.
  • Building relationships with employees, customers, and program partners.
  • Collaborating with a wide range of people from all walks of life.
  • Microsoft Office Suite.
  • Beginner skills in coding languages including: HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap.
